Problem Statement

Despite growing awareness about environmental issues, plastic waste continues to be a massive global problem. Over 8 million metric tons of plastic enter the oceans every year, harming marine life and ecosystems. Single-use plastics like bottles, bags, and packaging materials make up a significant portion of this waste. This pollution not only threatens marine species but also contributes to microplastic contamination, affecting food chains and human health.
